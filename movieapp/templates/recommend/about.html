About MovieMagnet

Welcome to MovieMagnet, a platform designed to provide personalized Movie based on collaborative filtering. Users can rate movies and add them to their watchlist.

How It Works

Our recommendation system analyzes user ratings and preferences to identify similar movies that users might enjoy. By leveraging collaborative filtering techniques, we strive to deliver accurate and relevant Movies tailored to each user's taste.

Features

  • Personalized Movies based on collaborative filtering
  • Users can rate movies to improve recommendation accuracy
  • Users can add movies to their watchlist for future viewing
